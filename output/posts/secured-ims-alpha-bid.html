We've secured our IMS Alpha bid!

Our project on Income Management and ePayment systems is progressing to the Alpha phase.

Published on 13 March 2020

IMS Alpha Income Management Local Digital Fund

We're absolutely thrilled to say that this afternoon we received confirmation that we'd succesfully bid to the Local Digital Fund and been awarded £100,000 to progress our project!

MHCLG announced the results on Twitter and you can read more about all of the 6 successful bids at https://www.gov.uk/government/news/councils-given-1-2-million-for-innovative-digital-projects.

We're really pleased with this and keen to get a kick-off meeting in with our partners ASAP.
